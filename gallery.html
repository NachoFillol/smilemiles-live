From Gower Park to the lake's edge — a Hamilton morning walk

We start where the locals do — at Gower Park on Alison Street, where the wide green fields and quiet paths make for a gentle warm-up. From there, the walk winds out through the leafy streets of the Hamilton Lake neighbourhood, past front gardens and unhurried corners that give a real feel for everyday life in this part of Waikato. The pace is easy and the streets are calm. When the trees clear, Lake Rotoroa opens up ahead — and the rest of the walk follows the famous 3.8 km lakeside loop, with ducks, pūkeko, and a boardwalk stretch to enjoy before a well-earned rest by the water.
